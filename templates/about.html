About Vintage

Hello, welcome to Vintage – The next generation photo editor. Trouble storing pictures, editing, adding effects? That’s what we are here to help you with. We provide various features to assist your photo needs. Few of them being
Gallery - Vintage lets you to easily store and view your pictures in one place.
Editing – With Vintage, you can now easily edit brightness, contrast, sharpness, blur, denoise resize & rotate according to your choice.
Effects – Vintage lets you choose from a number of predetermined effects which helps you change the coloring and look of your pictures.
